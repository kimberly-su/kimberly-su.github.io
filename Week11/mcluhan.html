The Medium is the Massage

Summary

In Marshall McLuhan's zine, The Medium is the Massage, he emphasizes that when messages are being conveyed, the medium is sometimes more significant than the actual message itself.

My Thoughts

McLuhan's insight really made me think about if it applied to the way I absorbed information provided to me. After thinking for a while, I definitely think it does. The best example I can think of is podcasts. For me, listening to a podcast versus watching a video of the people recording the podcast are entirely different experiences.
